Eligibility Criteria

General Requirements
  • Must be a full-time student enrolled in a recognized institution
  • Minimum GPA of 3.5 on a 4.0 scale
  • Must demonstrate financial need
  • Submit an official transcript and two letters of recommendation
Additional Requirements
  • Active involvement in extracurricular activities
  • Demonstrated leadership potential
  • Submission of a personal essay on future academic and career goals

How to Apply

Step 1: Complete the Application Form

Download the application form, fill it out completely, and submit it online or by email.

Step 2: Gather Documents

Submit your academic records, recommendation letters, and any other required documents.

Step 3: Interview

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview. The final decision will be based on merit.

Step 4: Receive Scholarship

Successful candidates will be notified and awarded the scholarship to fund their studies.

  • Opportunity to be a part of a vibrant scholarship community

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the application deadline?

The deadline for all scholarship applications is May 31st every year. Late submissions will not be accepted.

Can I apply for multiple scholarships?

Yes, you can apply for as many scholarships as you qualify for. However, you must meet the eligibility requirements for each.
